About Us

Welcome To Atlehang Productions

Little Story

We are a startup media company that creates new quality content and produce digital content for both television and online streaming.

We pride ourselves with the work of extra ordinary South African creatives who are selected for their strong work ethic, passion and expertise. The company has 50 employees. We are national company that entertains viewers with great stories told by South Africans. All the projects were created in both English and South African indigenous language.


Our services also include photography, music, videos, corporate videos and live performance

Founder: Annastasia Lehlakola

Annantasia Lehlakola is a founder of Atlehang Productions. She has studied TV and Firm production at Nemisa and has continued her studies with Boston Media House studying BBA in media operations management.

She has worked in Television for over 10 years and has worked in dynamic productions companies like Zola 7, Dzuguda productions and a broadcaster like Multichoice. She has done philanthropic work as she is passionate about helping others.

Vision and Mission Statement

Encourage innovation and creativity.

Become the leading production company that does not only entertain but empowers our South African woman to break out of their shells and be successful.
